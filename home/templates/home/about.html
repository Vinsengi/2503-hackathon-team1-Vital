Meet the Team

Inspiring Women in Tech

Our web app is designed to inspire the next generation of tech innovators by sharing the stories of women pioneers in technology. We aim to break barriers, close gender gaps, and build a diverse and inclusive tech community.

What We Do

  • Showcase the achievements of women in tech through a retro-inspired timeline.
  • Provide valuable tech tips to empower women in their careers.
  • Bridge the mentorship gap by connecting mentees with experienced mentors.

Our Design & Methodology

Built with Django, Tailwind CSS, and a touch of nostalgic Windows 98 style, our site blends modern technology with a retro aesthetic. We use an agile Kanban approach to continuously refine our user experience.

Future Vision

As we continue to grow, our roadmap includes exciting new features—enhanced storytelling tools, interactive timelines, and expanded mentorship programs—to further empower women in tech.

Our Team

We are a diverse team of passionate individuals dedicated to celebrating innovation and fostering growth in the tech community. Through collaboration and creativity, we aim to leave a lasting impact on the industry.
